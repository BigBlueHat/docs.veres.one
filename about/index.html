Governance

Board of Governors

The Board of Governors are responsible for governance of the Veres One Ledger. This includes ensuring the proper execution of the Veres One Project mission and managing, collecting, and distributing funds according to the funding model.

Maintainer

The Maintainer is responsible for maintaining the Veres One software including continuous security review, managing community contributions, correcting software defects, and implementing new features.

Costs

The Veres One Network operates as a non-profit so costs can be kept to absolute minimum. As the network scales the fees charges to the services will decrease. By not utilizing a network token currency speculation is avoided, ensuring long term cost predictability.

Revenue

The Veres One Project will receive revenue to fund the network in two forms, a flat fee for identifier creation and a percentage fee on what accelerators are charging to write a particular type of data to the network, such as digital credentials.
