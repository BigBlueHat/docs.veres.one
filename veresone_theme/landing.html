A Globally Interoperable Blockchain for Identity

Veres One is a secure, privacy preserving and cost effective way for you to own and manage your identity on the Web.

Get a Decentralized Identity!

Veres One is a fit-for-purpose blockchain optimized for identity on the Web.

Decentralized public key infrastructure for interoperable identity management at a global scale, implemented in the most cost effective, secure, and autonomous way possible.

Distributed

There is no central authority. Participants rely on consensus to make writes to the ledger, each preserving their own authority.

Tamper-proof and Auditable

Cryptographic ledgers are immutable; they provide high-integrity, robust, and dependable data, even when accessed when offline.

Autonomous

Veres One aims to maximise user autonomy, a.k.a Self Sovereign Identity, which means that any person or organization in the world can create and control their identifiers, aggregate data about themselves, and determine where that data is stored and who has access.

Low Cost

An optimized fit-for-purpose blockchain reduces computational overhead and therefore costs. Decentralization and cryptography increases cost sharing and resiliency in the face of failure.

Privacy Aware

User-centric architecture enables a person to reduce the amount of data shared with third parties ; they can selective choose which credentials, or even parts of credentials, are shared with whom.

Non-speculative

Unlike many other blockchain networks Veres One does not use a scarce network token. This removes price volatility as well as regulatory risk.

The Veres One Network is permissionless.
Anyone can participate!

Global Public Utility

Veres One is a global public utility for individuals, enterprises, and governments.

Individuals

Veres One is designed to put people in control of who and when their identity information is shared. It’s architecture also helps people resist censorship. Individuals can aggregate digital credentials from various parties.

Enterprises

Enterprises get a cost effective identity utility that is interoperable with anyone else that uses Veres One blockchain. Employees can seamlessly access restricted data without the need for usernames and passwords. Organizations can create dynamic identity management rules with ease.

Governments

Whether it be national governments, smart cities, or other municipalities, Veres One provides globally interoperable infrastructure that can streamline identity management while protecting the privacy of citizens.

Sectors

Government

Governments can issue digital identification such as driver’s licenses, passports, metro passes, food stamp vouchers, or for online voting. Citizens control who their identity information is shared with and when.

Healthcare

Veres One provides a common platform for disparate entities within the healthcare system to to interact with high assurance. Physician licenses, digital prescriptions, and medical records are just some examples of identity data that can be effortlessly verified.

Financial Services

Banks, credit unions, and other financial institutions benefit from stronger identity verification and cost effective know-your - customer checks, reducing fraud and easing regulatory compliance.

Underserved

Over one billion people in the world are unable to prove their identity, restricting access to financial services, education, and healthcare. Developing countries and service organizations can use Veres One to provide a root identity enables access services and helps coordinate aid.

Trade

Trade is a vast and complex segment of the economy consisting of many entities. These entities can be more strongly authenticated along entire supply chains, reducing friction in identity management and fraud.

Social

Social apps including decentralized social networks., peer to peer messaging, and sharing economy apps can benefit from using Veres One. It enables maximum user centricity and data portability.

Become and Accelerator

Accelerators can write information such as credentials to the Veres One Ledger in just a few seconds.

If you want to create new services or integrate your products with the Veres One Ledger you can either write to the ledger through an existing Accelerator or become an Accelerator.

Accelerators include registrars, governments credential issuers and digital wallet providers.

Anyone can become an accelerator as long as they abide by the simple Accelerator Agreement

Run a Node

Running a node of the Veres One Ledger provides instant access to data, onsite backups, and money!

A sufficient number of nodes run by a variety of entities is essential for the health and security of the network. Nodes are financially compensated by the Veres One Project for their contribution to the network.

As the system is permissionless, any person or organization may run one or more Nodes if they follow the guidelines in the Simple Node Agreement.

How it works

It takes about 3 minutes to get a digital wallet and a Veres One identifier.

Issuers issue credentials to Veres One identifiers and wallets under the wallet owner’s control. The Wallet owner can then present those credentials to verifiers without the need for issuer notification.

Wallet owners gather credentials issued from various third parties and store them in digital wallets. Wallet owners share credentials with verifiers they choose in order to achieve their objective.

Wallet owners store their digital credentials on their devices for future use..

Ecosystem

Veres One Project

The Veres One Project is a not-for-profit entity that maintains and provides governance for the Veres One Network. It receives revenue from accelerators to pay for maintenance, upgrades, and security of the network.

Accelerators

Accelerators create identifiers on the Veres One Blockchain for customers and compensate the Veres One Project to fund network operations. Anyone may be an accelerator as long as they abide by the accelerator agreement.

Nodes

Nodes are responsible for providing compute and storage resources that keep the Network secure. As the system is permissionless, any person or organization may run one or more Nodes. Nodes are compensated by the Veres One Project
